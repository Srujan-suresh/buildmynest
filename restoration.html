Our Services

Preserve Heritage, Restore Glory

BuildMyNest specializes in restoring and revitalizing aging properties, heritage buildings, and structures that have been damaged by time, weather, or neglect. Our restoration services combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to preserve architectural integrity while ensuring structural safety and longevity.

Our Restoration Services

Heritage Building Restoration

Careful restoration of historic properties maintaining original architectural features, period-appropriate materials, and traditional construction methods while meeting modern safety standards.

Structural Repair & Strengthening

Expert assessment and repair of damaged foundations, cracked walls, weakened beams, and structural instability using advanced engineering solutions.

Waterproofing & Seepage Treatment

Comprehensive waterproofing solutions for roofs, terraces, basements, and bathrooms. Treatment of dampness, seepage, and water infiltration issues.

Facade Restoration

Cleaning, repair, and restoration of building exteriors including stonework, brickwork, plaster, decorative elements, and architectural details.

Wood & Metalwork Restoration

Restoration of antique doors, windows, wooden beams, metal railings, grills, and decorative elements through repair, refinishing, and protective treatments.

Earthquake & Disaster Rehabilitation

Post-disaster assessment and restoration of damaged structures with seismic strengthening and disaster-resistant upgrades.

Our Restoration Process

  1. Detailed Assessment: Comprehensive structural survey, damage analysis, material testing, and documentation of existing conditions.
  2. Conservation Plan: Development of restoration strategy preserving historical significance while ensuring structural safety.
  3. Material Sourcing: Procurement of period-appropriate or matching materials, traditional lime mortars, authentic finishes.
  4. Skilled Execution: Work by specialized craftsmen using traditional techniques combined with modern technology.
  5. Quality Monitoring: Regular inspections by conservation experts and structural engineers throughout the restoration.
  6. Documentation & Handover: Complete records of restoration work, maintenance guidelines, and warranty certificates.

Why Choose BuildMyNest for Restoration

  • • Heritage Expertise: Specialized knowledge in traditional construction and conservation
  • • Skilled Craftsmen: Team trained in traditional techniques and restoration methods
  • • Structural Safety: Modern engineering solutions ensuring long-term stability
  • • Authentic Materials: Access to traditional and period-appropriate building materials
  • • Regulatory Compliance: Assistance with heritage permits and conservation approvals
  • • Long-term Warranty: Comprehensive guarantees on structural work and waterproofing
